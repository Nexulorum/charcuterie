Privacy Policy

How StroGraze may collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website.

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit an order request or contact form, StroGraze may collect information such as your name, phone number, email address, pickup or delivery details, event information, dietary notes, allergy notes, and other information you choose to provide.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to requests, confirm order details, provide pickup or delivery information, communicate about custom charcuterie orders, and manage customer service needs.

Order Forms and Website Hosting

This website may use third-party hosting and form handling services, including Netlify, to receive and process form submissions. Information submitted through website forms may be processed by those service providers as part of normal website operation.

Payments

Payments may be handled through secure third-party payment processors, including Stripe. StroGraze does not store full payment card numbers on this website. Payment information is handled according to the payment provider’s own security and privacy practices.

Cookies and Basic Website Data

This website may use basic cookies, hosting logs, or analytics tools to understand general website performance, traffic, and usage. This information may include browser type, device type, pages visited, and general interaction data.

Sharing Information

StroGraze does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the website, process payments, fulfill orders, communicate with customers, comply with legal obligations, or protect business rights.

Allergy and Dietary Information

Allergy and dietary notes are used only to review and prepare order requests. Customers are responsible for providing accurate information before an order is finalized.

Data Security

We use reasonable steps to protect submitted information, but no website, form, email, or payment system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Data Retention

Order and contact information may be retained as needed for communication, customer service, business records, and legal or accounting purposes.

Your Choices

You may contact StroGraze to request updates or deletion of information you previously submitted, subject to any information that must be retained for business, legal, or accounting reasons.
